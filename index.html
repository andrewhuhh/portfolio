re/branding case study

Strataigize is a results-oriented app marketing agency that specializes in App Store Optimization (ASO) for mobile apps. they were looking for a visual refresh to help reposition themselves as a more reputable leader in the app marketing space.

I was hired to be their Creative Lead and oversee a comprehensive rebranding + web redesign in an effort to increase their organic traffic, sales and reputation.
